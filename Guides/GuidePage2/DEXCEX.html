Which is a better exchange?

What are DEXs?

A Decentralised Crypto Exchange (DEX) is a peer-to-peer (P2P) marketplace that connects cryptocurrency buyers and sellers

What are CEXs?

A Centralised Crypto Exchange(CEX) is an exchange that is created and run by one company that oversees all the transactions and sets the exchange's rules and fees.
