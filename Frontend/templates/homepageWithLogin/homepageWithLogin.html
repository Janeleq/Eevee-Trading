About us

Cryptocurrency trading can be daunting to enter for many people especially when it is such a volatile market. Many have lost their fortune on crypto trading due to negligence and lack of knowledge on what they are investing in and steep learning curve. However, the sky's the limit when you do crypto trading.

There are many who are successful in their trading and resulted in them bringing themselves out of poverty. Thus we aim to equip beginners with sufficient knowledge to take the first step towards crypto trading as well as to help them understand the gains and pains of crypto trading through our simplified cryptocurrency trading simulator for users to get hands-on practice.
